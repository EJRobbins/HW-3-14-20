Welcome to my page!

My name is Erica. I'm a 19 year old who studies at the University of Utah in Sandy. I never imagined myself coding. Before I took up any sort of development, I managed a coffee shop and changed majors twice before I even turned 18. I started studying Biology, then moved to Psychology. I loved them both, but something was wrong. I didn't feel challenged, and I didn't feel as if I was making any sort of impact on the world. After years of coffee and indecisiveness, I quit my job and moved across town to start at a softare development company in Lehi. This is where I learned my love of development and the quick pace of tech! On this website, you'll find both my contact information, and some of my portfolio projects! I truly hope you enjoy my little corner of the internet!
